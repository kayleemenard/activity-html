Thousands of Swamp Things

Conventional wisdom tells us there are predominantly two movies out there:

  1. Swamp Thing
  2. The Return Of Swamp Thing (its less popular but still well-known follow-up)
What we’re finding, however, is that those two films are just the tip of the iceberg in terms of what the art of cinema has to offer.

What does this mean???

According to the report, most movies aren’t even based on DC Comics superheroes—or on comics at all, for that matter—but exist instead within completely unrelated genres:

  • Romantic Comedies
  • Period Dramas
  • Animated Features
  • Musicals
  • and even Documentaries

“As for the quality of these other films, however…” AFI board of trustees chair Howard added. “Well, I really can’t speak to that.”
